Ugali Recipe

Description

This is the african to specific staple food of Kenya, it consists majorly of carbohydrates and cooked withn 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1KG of Maize Flour
  • 3 Cups of water
  • Magarine

Steps

  1. Boil 3 cups of water in a sufuria
  2. Add 1Kg of maize flour once the water is boiling
  3. Stir the mixture until it is thick solid
  4. Keep stiring the mixtire till it stops sticking on the mwiko

Congratulations, your ugali is ready to be served, place the whole ugali on a plate, and serve while cutting like a cake
